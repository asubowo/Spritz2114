Welcome to Quick Reader, a group project greatly based on the techniques by Spritz.

How the heck does Spritz or Quick Reader work?

Essentially, Quick Reader attempts to center every word around a common area, reducing the amount of time your eyes have to 'travel' in order to read the entire word. By having your eyes just center and zone in on a common word, we can accelerate the words shown per minute and effectively comprehend what's being displayed.

How do I install this?

This app will never be released on Google Play. You are more than welcome to download the project and compile it yourself to generate the .apk.
